About Darou Gostaran Bahrami

Bridging Pharmaceutical Gaps in the Middle East

Darou Gostaran Bahrami is a new and rapidly growing company designed to fill the gaps in providing pharmaceutical products throughout the Middle East. Headquartered in Tehran, we are licensed under Iran's Food and Drug Administration and work closely with the agency to secure appropriate licensing for drug importation.

The company is quickly expanding, with plans for branches in Armenia and Iraq starting in 2026 and beyond. Darou Gostaran Bahrami also collaborates with Iran's Food and Drug Administration to import pharmaceutical drugs on the urgently needed list, ensuring timely access to critical medications.
