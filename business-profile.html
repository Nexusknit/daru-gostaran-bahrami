Business Profile

Business Profile

Introduction

Darou Gostaran Bahrami Ltd. is a rapidly expanding company, recently founded and licensed under national ID 14014153802. Our core focus is the importation of pharmaceutical products into the Middle East. Licensed by Iran's FDA, we also distribute medicines, medical devices and equipment to pharmacies across Iran. Headquarters are in Tehran, with a purchasing office in Los Angeles and Warsaw. We are building partnerships in Armenia and Iraq, and Dr Bita Bahrami PharmD is our technical manager and primary liaison with Iran's FDA.

Mission Statement

  • Provide high-quality, timely pharmaceutical products across Iran and the wider Middle East.
  • Build long-term relationships with suppliers and customers through professional communication and excellent performance.
  • Respond proactively to rapid changes in pharmaceutical supply and demand.
  • Continuously improve our services and customer experience.

Vision

A young, vibrant team located worldwide is ready to meet the region’s pharmaceutical challenges.

Philosophy

Our philosophy is constant improvement and expansion; our commitment is the highest quality service. Client satisfaction is our goal—bringing the latest advances in pharmaceuticals to the Middle East without barriers.
