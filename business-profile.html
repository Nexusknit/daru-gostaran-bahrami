Business Profile

Introduction

Darou Gostaran Bharami Ltd, is a rapidly expanding company that was founded very recently. It is a pharmaceutical company licensed under the national ID number of 14014153802, primarily focused on importation of pharmaceutical products internationally into the Middle East. It is licensed for pharmaceutical drug importation into Iran under Iranian food and drug administration supervision. Our company is also licensed to distribute pharmaceutical drugs, medical devices and medical equipment to sales companies or pharmacies across Iran. The headquarter of our firm is located in Tehran while we have our purchase manager based in Los Angeles as well as Warsaw. The company is in contact with potential business partners in Armenia and Iraq and looking to expand across the Middle East. Dr Bita Bahrami PharmD is our technical manager and our contact with the Iranian food and drug administration.

Mission Statement

  • Provide high-quality and timely pharmaceutical products to customers across Iran and the wider Middle East.
  • Create long-term business relationships with suppliers and customers through professional communication and satisfactory performance.
  • Respond proactively to rapid changes in supply and demand within the pharmaceutical industry.
  • Strive to improve our services and customer experience.

Vision

We have a young and vibrant team who are based internationally and across Iran. We stand ready to respond to the needs and challenges of the pharmaceutical customers across the Middle East.

Mission

Our philosophy is constant improvement, growth and expansion. Our commitment is highest quality service to our customers. Client satisfaction is our goal and our aim is to break down the traditional barriers and provide access to the latest advances of pharmaceutical industry across the Middle East.
